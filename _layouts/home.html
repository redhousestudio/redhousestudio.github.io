Pre Production

We’ll talk, listen to demos and find what is important for your music. How to present your music to your listeners. Prepare, fine tune and practice performances.
I will plan the production to materialize your vision.

Production

Construct and conceive all the musical layers. Recording whatever anywhere that fits. I can assist with composing, arrangements, sounddesign, help you perform your songs and make a snapshot of your peak performance.

Mix

With the recorded material we’ll sit in the lab and edit takes, add FX and mix it to enhance the story lines. Balance the position of all sounds. We'll paint a picture for your audience.

Mastering

I can do the mastering or we send the mix to a mastering studio of our choice but I will make sure that in the end everybody is happy.
